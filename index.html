About Us

Manja Youth is a Blantyre-based organization founded in 2020. Our mission is to create a world full of expected wows, spotlighting hidden talents, and telling the stories that must be told through arts, music, film, books, and community efforts.

Our Sub-Boards

  • K.K Productions: U.S.-based production house inspired by Tamanda Kadewere. Specializes in storytelling, audio, and documentaries.
  • V.N.T: Venture New Talents. Focuses on discovering and nurturing new talent.
  • Moonlight: A bridge for all film and arts production. Links other film-based initiatives like Kwathu Studio.
  • Highliner Studio: Music-focused sub-board helping youth grow in musical skill and creativity.
  • Kwathu Creative Arts: Home to true, youth-focused stories in ebook, audiobook, and narrative forms. First project: Tikaimba.
  • Home & Green: Advocates for sustainable growth and environmental care in local communities.
  • CO & MAX: Final publishing and article production hub for creative works.

Books

Our debut book Tikaimba is a powerful, emotionally rich novel exploring youth, sexuality, music, and biblical reflection. More books coming soon.
